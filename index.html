Hugo Ballester is the playful alter ego of the more serious Hugo Zaragoza, a machine learning scientist, engineer and entrepeneur.

Ballester is Hugo's mother's name, a name with a long tradition of painters and sculptors, such as Bruno, AnaRosa, Jorge, Manuela y Antonio.
